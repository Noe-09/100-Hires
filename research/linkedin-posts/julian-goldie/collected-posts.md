### Raw content

I built an SEO article in under 10 minutes using AI.

Here’s how👇

Most people use 1 AI tool and pray.

That’s not a strategy.

That’s SEO roulette with extra WiFi.

Use this 3-tool system instead:

1️⃣ Perplexity = research

Search your topic.

Ask for keywords, top pages, questions, and content gaps.

It gives you live data + citations.

2️⃣ NotebookLM = brain

Paste all the research inside.

Ask it to turn the mess into a clean SEO outline.

Now your content has structure.

Fancy word for “not spaghetti.”

3️⃣ Gemini = writer

Import your NotebookLM notes.

Ask Gemini to write the full SEO article.

Add headers, keywords, examples, links, and clear answers.

4️⃣ Edit before posting

Add your voice.

Add screenshots.

Add internal links.

Add proof.

Do not publish robot soup.

5️⃣ Repeat weekly

Research.

Organize.

Write.

Publish.

That’s how you turn AI into a content machine.

Not magic.

Just a better system.

### Research notes

- Main idea: AI can speed up SEO content production when used as a workflow, not just as a writing shortcut.
- Useful for future playbook: Good example for a “fast AI SEO workflow” section.
- Tactical insight: Separate research, outlining, drafting, and optimization into different steps/tools.
- Risk / limitation: Fast production can create low-quality content if human review and fact-checking are skipped.
