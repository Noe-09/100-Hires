# AI-Powered SEO Content Production for B2B SaaS

## Overview

This project is a research collection about how B2B SaaS companies can use AI in their SEO content workflow.

I chose this topic because SEO and content are important growth channels for many SaaS companies. AI can help with research, outlines, research collection about how B2B SaaS companies can use AI in their SEO content workflow.

I chose this topic drafts, editing, optimization, and repurposing, but it still needs human judgment to keep the content useful and accurate.

The goal of this repository is not to create a final playbook yet. The goal is to collect and organize strong sources from people who actually work in SEO, content marketing, AI search, and B2B SaaS growth.

## Why I Chose This Topic

100Hires works in a content-heavy B2B SaaS category. Topics like applicant tracking, hiring workflows, sourcing, interview process, recruiting automation, and ATS comparisons can all be supported by useful SEO content.

Because of that, I thought AI-powered SEO content production would be a relevant topic to research. It connects growth marketing, SEO, content operations, AI tools, and technical workflows.

I also wanted to avoid treating AI content as just “generate more articles faster.” I’m more interested in how AI can support a better content process while still keeping quality, accuracy, and usefulness.

## What I Collected

This repository includes:

- A list of 10 experts and why I chose them
- LinkedIn posts organized by author
- YouTube transcripts organized by video
- Other useful materials such as newsletters, podcasts, or blog posts
- Notes on early patterns I noticed while collecting the sources
